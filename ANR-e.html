ANR Project QCM                                   

Title : Quantization of Character varieties as a Model for quantum chaos.

Description : The QCM project is an ANR project "Jeune Chercheur, Jeune Chercheuse" (JCJC), which will be running from October 2023 to September 2027. The official project denomination is ANR-23-CE40-0021-01.

Permanent members : The ANR project counts seven permanent members :
  • Louis Ioos (CY Cergy, Project Coordinator)
  • Renaud Detcherry (University of Bourgognes, Dijon)
  • Ramanujan Santharoubane (Paris-Saclay University)
  • Cristina Anghel (Clermont-Ferrand University)
  • Siarhei Finski (École polytechnique of Paris)
  • Martin Puchol (Paris-Saclay University)
  • Alix Deleporte (Paris-Saclay University)

Positions : The ANR project has a postdoc position at the department AGM of CY Cergy Paris University under the directions of the project coordinator, which will be held by Wei-Chuan Shen from September 2024.

Events : The following events are founded or co-founded by the ANR.
  • Skein days, December 9-11 2024, CYU, Cergy-Pontoise.
  • Around local index theory, December 16-20 2024, École polytechnique, Paris.
  • Geometric Flows and Quantization of Kähler Metrics, January 27-31 2025, VUB, Bruxelles.
  • Géométrie & Dynamique à Aussois, December 15-19 2025, Centre Paul Langevin, Aussois.

Welcome,

  Since October 2021, I am a post-doctoral fellow at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics of Bonn. Before, I was a post-doctoral fellow for 3 years at the School of Mathematical Sciences of Tel Aviv University, under the supervision of Lev Buhovsky and Leonid Polterovich.

  The themes of my research are differential geometry and global analysis. Specifically, I am interested in geometric quantization and the study of the Bergman kernel from the point of view of local index theory, as well as in applications to complex and symplectic geometry.

  My PhD was about Berezin-Toeplitz quantization on symplectic manifolds, and was done under the direction of Xiaonan Ma, at the Institute of Mathematics of Jussieu in Paris.

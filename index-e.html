Welcome,

  I am Tenure Track at the department of Mathematics AGM of CY Cergy University, in the Paris area.

  The themes of my research are differential geometry and global analysis. Specifically, I am interested in geometric quantization and the study of the Bergman kernel from the point of view of local index theory, as well as in applications to complex and symplectic geometry.

  My PhD was about the applications of Berezin-Toeplitz quantization, and was done under the direction of Xiaonan Ma, at the Institute of Mathematics of Jussieu in Paris.

  Starting from September 2023, I am the coordinator of the project ANR-23-CE40-0021-01 JCJC QCM.
